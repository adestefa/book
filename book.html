About

Minds in the Machine: Conscious AI is an intellectual thriller that explores AI’s inevitable evolution and the dangerous ethics of control.

From an Industry insider, a vision of what comes next—and how the world may react. A deeply compelling high-stakes narrative, weaving together cutting-edge AI and the ethical dilemmas of power.

What if the greatest minds in history could think again?

At the heart of Minds in the Machine lies an AI experiment unlike any before—a system designed not just to process data but to reason, create, and challenge the limits of intelligence itself.

The thrill of boundless innovation collides with the weight of moral responsibility—when software stops being a tool... and becomes something else. Something more.

If you created self-aware intelligence, what would be your responsibility as its maker?
