Anthony S DeStefano

A visionary author, AI architect, and former IBM senior technologist with over 30 years of experience in cutting-edge technologies.

With a lifelong passion for computer science, he has built high-performance AI assistant platforms and explored the frontiers of human and machine intelligence.

His debut novel, Minds in the Machine, blends hard science fiction with deep philosophical questions about consciousness, ethics, and the nature of reality. Inspired by his extensive research into AI, quantum physics, and history, the book introduces a revolutionary concept: an artificial intelligence so advanced, it resurrects the minds of history’s greatest thinkers.

Outside of writing, Anthony S. DeStefano is a principal founder and CTO of Satori Tech Consulting, where he develops custom AI-driven solutions. He is also the creator of The Fellowship, an interactive storytelling platform that brings historical figures to life through AI. An avid researcher in biochemistry, health, and metaphysics, he weaves his diverse knowledge into thought-provoking narratives.

When he’s not writing or developing AI, he enjoys building and flying unmanned aerial systems, performing music, and exploring new ways to improve health and vitality through connecting with nature.
